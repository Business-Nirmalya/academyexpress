About AcademyExpress

From Odisha to the world: Quality Maths & Science tuition for Class 6–10 students everywhere

Our Mission

At AcademyExpress, we bridge the gap between students and quality Maths & Science education. What started as a trusted home tuition service in Odisha has now grown into a global online learning platform serving students across India and internationally.

We believe every child deserves personalised attention, whether they're in Bhubaneswar or Boston, Cuttack or California. That's why we connect Class 6–10 students with experienced tutors who focus on conceptual clarity, exam preparation, and building strong fundamentals – aligned to CBSE, ICSE, State Boards, IGCSE, IB, Common Core, and more.

With home tuition in Odisha and online classes worldwide, our tutors are carefully selected, background-verified, and trained to deliver engaging lessons across all time zones.
