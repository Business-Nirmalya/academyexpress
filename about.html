About AcademyExpress

Odisha's trusted platform for Maths & Science tuition (Class 6–10)

Our Mission

At AcademyExpress, we bridge the gap between students and quality Maths & Science education across Odisha. Founded with a vision to make excellent tutoring accessible in every city — from Bhubaneswar to Rourkela, Cuttack to Sambalpur.

We believe every child deserves personalised attention. That's why we connect Class 6–10 students with experienced tutors who focus on conceptual clarity, board exam preparation, and building strong fundamentals for future competitive exams.

With both home tuition and online classes, we cater to CBSE, ICSE, and Odisha State Board curricula. Our tutors are carefully selected, background-verified, and trained to deliver engaging lessons.
